What's Jackbox Games?

Jackbox Games is a video game developer known for creating party games that are designed to be played in groups, often using smartphones as controllers. Founded in 2013, the company gained popularity with its Jackbox Party Pack series, which includes a variety of mini-games that can be played by multiple players, typically in a social setting. These games often feature interactive and humorous elements, making them suitable for gatherings, family events, and online play. By combining trivia, drawing, and other types of gameplay, Jackbox Games has established a reputation for fun and engaging experiences that encourage player interaction.
